# Homework & Assignment Strategies

## Setting Up for Success

### Your Study Environment
- **Dedicated spot** — Same place every day, with good lighting and a clear surface
- **No distractions** — Phone in another room (or airplane mode), TV off, notifications silenced
- **Everything ready** — All supplies within arm's reach before you start
- **Comfortable but alert** — A desk or table (not your bed!) with a supportive chair

### The Homework Routine
1. **Unpack and plan (5 min):** List every assignment. Estimate time for each.
2. **Hardest first:** Tackle the most challenging subject while your brain is fresh.
3. **Break between subjects:** 5-minute break between each subject to reset.
4. **Check your work:** Before closing a textbook, review your answers.
5. **Pack up:** Put everything in your bag immediately. Don't leave it "for the morning."

## Tackling Big Projects

### The Backward Planning Method
1. Start with the due date
2. Work backward: What needs to happen the day before? The week before?
3. Break the project into 5-7 smaller tasks
4. Assign each task a specific day on your calendar
5. Build in buffer days for unexpected problems

### Example: Book Report Due Friday
- Monday: Choose book, read chapters 1-3
- Tuesday: Read chapters 4-7, take notes
- Wednesday: Finish book, outline report
- Thursday: Write first draft
- Thursday evening: Revise and proofread
- Friday morning: Final check before turning in

## When You're Stuck
- **Re-read the instructions** — 90% of confusion comes from skimming directions
- **Check your notes** — Look for examples from class
- **Try for 10 minutes** — Give it a real effort before asking for help
- **Ask for help** — Teacher office hours, study groups, parent, online resources
- **Move on and return** — Sometimes a break makes hard problems easier

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Waiting until the night before for big assignments
- Doing homework with the TV on or while texting
- Rushing through to "just get it done" without understanding
- Not checking your work before submitting
- Forgetting to turn in completed work (put it in your bag immediately!)
